⚠️ Warning️

I know a lot of people like to put the water directly into the cup and then microwave it but this is extremely dangerous. Maruchan, Cup of Noodles, and many other ramen cups use styrofoam cups that melt when placed in the microwave that leak into the water and into your food. These chemicals are carcinogenic so do not do this. You may even use a microwave safe cup and put water in it for it to boil but never put the water in the ramen cup then microwave
